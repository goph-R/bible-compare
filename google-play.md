## Full description (4000 chars max)
Bible Compare lets you read and compare Bible verses across multiple translations side by side — completely offline, with no account required.

Switch instantly between translations using tabs while your reading position is preserved. Highlight any verse with a tap, bookmark your favourites, and search through the full text of any translation with accent-insensitive matching.

Features:
- 4 bundled translations: KJV, Bible in Basic English, Karoli (Hungarian), and Original (Hebrew OT + Greek NT)
- Tabbed interface — switch translations with one tap, verse position is preserved
- Full-text search with highlighted results and paging
- Bookmarks — save and navigate to favourite verses
- Dark and light theme — follows your system preference or toggle manually
- Right-to-left support for Hebrew text
- Bilingual UI — English and Hungarian with automatic detection
- 100% offline — all data is bundled, no internet required
- No ads, no tracking, no account needed

Perfect for Bible study, devotional reading, or comparing how different translations render the same passage.
